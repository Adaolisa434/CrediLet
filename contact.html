Frequently Asked Questions

How do I verify my property listing?

+

Our admin team reviews all property listings before they go live. You'll need to provide property documents, photos, and complete our verification process.

Is CrediLet free for renters?

+

Yes! CrediLet is completely free for renters. You can browse listings, chat with agents, and schedule inspections at no cost.

How do I become a verified agent?

+

Complete our KYC process by providing your identification, professional credentials, and references. Our team will review and verify your profile.

What areas do you currently serve?

+

We're currently focused on Lagos, Nigeria. We're planning to expand to other major Nigerian cities soon.
